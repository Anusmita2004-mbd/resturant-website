Restaurant Services

Master every step of excellent restaurant service

1. Guest Reception

  • Welcome within 30 seconds
  • Smile & friendly tone
  • Escort to the table
  • Assist elderly, families, etc.
  • Prompt menu delivery
  • Highlight specials or chef's picks

2. Order Taking

  • Ready notepad and pen
  • Accurate repeat order
  • Respect order decision
  • Allergy check

3. Service Delivery

  • Appetizers in 10 mins
  • Main course in 20 mins
  • Neat, appealing plating
  • Check back within 5 minutes

4. Beverage Service

  • Use correct type for each drink
  • Pour at table when possible
  • Act proactively before glass is empty

5. Table Maintenance

  • Prompt & discreet clearing
  • Crumbing between courses
  • Prepare for next course efficiently

6. Handling Complaints

  • Listen: Let guest speak fully
  • Apologize: Sincere and respectful
  • Resolve: Act quickly and efficiently

7. Event Catering & Special Arrangements

  • Customized menu planning
  • Efficient coordination
  • Timely setup and delivery
  • Accept and deliver large meal orders for birthday, office events

8. Closing Procedures

  • Final Check – Tables cleared/reset
  • Billing – Prompt, professional
  • Farewell – Thank & invite back
